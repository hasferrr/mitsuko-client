### Cat 4: Button size variant overrides

**`user.tsx:163-165`** — `<Button size="sm" className="py-0 px-2 h-6">`

- `size="sm"` gives h-7, gap-1, px-2.5 — but `h-6 px-2` override everything back to xs dimensions
- `size="xs"` provides h-6, gap-1, px-2 natively
- Fix: `size="xs" className="py-0"` — remove `px-2` and `h-6` (provided by xs), keep `py-0`

### Cat 7: Sibling spacing via space-y

- Parent `<div className="w-[min(42rem,90vw)] mx-auto">` has no `space-y-*`
- Fix: Add `space-y-6` to parent div, remove `mb-6` from Card at line 122 and `mt-6` from Card at line 353
